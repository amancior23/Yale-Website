No Hypotheticals

Michael Ndubisi is a writer, organizer, and advocate. He serves as an organizer on the central committees for the Monterey County Democratic Party and California State Democratic Party. His work and reporting have appeared in the Yale Daily News, The Politic, Voices of Monterey Bay and other publications as well as winning him a Vice Youth Poet Laureate Award. Ndubisi has organized around many issues with various grassroots organizations including March For Our Lives, One Fair Wage, Justice Impact Alliance, Grassroots Democrats and many others.
